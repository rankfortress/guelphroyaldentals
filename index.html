Guelph Royal Dental Centre

At Guelph Royal Dental Centre, we are committed to providing comprehensive dental care for the entire family.We offer a wide range of services, including periodontal surgeries and treatment, as well as dental implants. Offering most procedures in house means patients can receive treatment as promptly as possible. By keeping our services under one roof, we are able cut down the time a patient waits for their treatment, making your experience in our clinic seamless. Our practice is fully digitized, making your visit to our office as efficient as possible. All patient files are digital and we utilize digital x-rays and ortho-panoramic radiographs. Additionally, during treatment our doctors utilize intraoral camera technology, allowing you to see exactly what they see in a diagnosis.
